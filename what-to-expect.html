  2. What To Expect

We're here to make you feel most comfortable so you can enjoy your experience.

Before we get started, we will provide an assessment to find out more information about your needs, and concerns. You will wear soft movable clothing, or undress to your level of comfort, depending ont he kind of work we will be doing in session. When undressed, before I re-enter the room, you will be fully covered with a sheet that will be provided from us. Only individual parts of the body will be undraped to work specific areas, then re-draped when finished. You will lie down on the comfortable massage table, feeling safe, and empowered by the information you've received, and knowledgeable about the plan of action we will take during your session. The parts of your body that may be touched include the head, neck, face, chest, stomach, hands, back, gluteal region, legs and feet. Please do keep in communication with us about areas of the body you may need focused work; as well as any request to not be touched in various areas at all.

This experience is about you, your body, and your healing, so it is important that you communicate any discomfort or needs that may arise in session. Some clients may experience an emotional release during or after receiving bodywork. This is not uncommon and is acceptable. We are here to support you, and maintain a safe, positive space during your session. When the session is over, you will redress and we will do a reassessment to monitor your progress from the days session. I will also offer helpful information to assist you with self care, and general wellness.
