Swedish Massage

Swedish Massage is a very relaxing, stress relieving, and nurturing style of bodywork. Every massage is personalized. Swedish massage blends flowing, rolling, kneading, and circulatory strokes to restore calm and relief of aches and pains, while increasing circulation and greater range of motion throughout your body.

Sports Massage

Sports Massage is a type of massage designed for highly active, athletic people. Sports Massage enhances performance and prolongs a sports career by helping to prevent injury, reduce pains and swelling in the body, relax the mind, increase flexibility, and dramatically improve recovery rates. Sports Massage is also highly effective in aiding the rapid recovery of an athlete from an injury by encouraging greater kinesthetic awareness.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep Tissue Massage is a form of bodywork that is focused and reparative. Open communication between client and therapist is a must during the session to ensure your comfort level. The goal is to target tension in specific areas in the deeper layers of tissue in the body. Deep Tissue Massage is a highly effective method of bodywork for releasing chronic stress areas due to misalignment, overuse, repetitive motions, past injuries, and increase range of motion in the targeted areas.

Myofascial Therapy

Myofascial Therapy is very specific work. We will go through a range of movement assessment, and then address the specific problem. Myofascial bodywork is slow with intent. Focusing on fibrotic tissue and misaligned muscle fibers and re- educating them into a healthy state. Including proprioceptive neuromuscular facilitation stretching. Myofascial therapy releases tension and pain, promotes increase range of motion, and posture realignment.

Pregnacy Massage

Pregnancy Massage is a style of bodywork that is mindful of your extraordinary condition. Using various techniques appropriate for your stage of pregnancy, Pregnancy Massage helps release the pain and discomfort experienced throughout pregnancy. The benefits are profound, including emotional support, the relief of joint pain due to extra weight and postural imbalance, and improved breathing and relaxation.
